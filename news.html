FAQ’S

What time should I arrive for race day?
We recommend arriving at least 30–45 minutes before the first race to find parking, explore the venue, and enjoy the pre-race atmosphere. Check the event schedule for specific race start times.
Are there any age restrictions?
All ages are welcome at Fraser Trak! However, children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult. For specific race day activities, age restrictions may apply
Do you offer parking?
Yes, we have ample parking on-site. General parking is free, and we offer premium parking options closer to the track for those who prefer more convenience.
Is Fraser Trak family-friendly?
Absolutely! We provide family-friendly areas, activities for children, and a relaxed atmosphere where everyone can enjoy their time at the races.
Can I bring my own food and drinks?
Outside food and beverages are not allowed. However, our venue offers a variety of dining options, from quick bites to full meals, catering to all preferences.
What should I wear?
There’s no strict dress code, but many guests enjoy dressing up, especially in premium seating areas. Comfortable footwear is recommended for moving around the venue.
Is the venue accessible for individuals with disabilities?
Yes, Fraser Trak is fully accessible, with designated seating, parking, and restrooms for guests with disabilities. Please contact us in advance for any specific accommodations.
