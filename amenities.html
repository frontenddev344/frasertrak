Club Amenities

At Fraser Trak Pony Club, we are dedicated to ensuring an exceptional experience for all our members and visitors. Our wide range of amenities is designed to make your time at the club enjoyable, comfortable, and memorable. Explore what we have to offer:

Seating Options

  • General Seating: Enjoy the excitement of our equestrian activities from our comfortable general seating areas. Whether you prefer shaded spots or open spaces, our seating options offer excellent views of the arenas. Experience the thrill of the action in a comfortable setting that enhances your enjoyment.
  • Premium Seating: Enhance your visit with our premium seating options, which provide superior views and additional comfort. Premium seats often include access to exclusive areas and added conveniences, ensuring a luxurious experience for every guest.
  • Family-Friendly Areas: Create special moments with your family in our designated family-friendly zones. These areas feature interactive activities and amenities for children, ensuring that the entire family can enjoy their day at the club. Our family zones make it easy for kids to stay entertained while you relax and enjoy the events.

Dining and Refreshments

  • Concessions: Satisfy your hunger with a variety of quick and delicious options available at our concession stands. From classic snacks like sandwiches and pastries to healthier choices and refreshing drinks, there’s something to please every palate. We also cater to dietary preferences to ensure everyone finds something they love.
  • Café: For a more relaxed dining experience, visit our on-site café. Choose from a range of light meals and beverages while enjoying the view of our equestrian facilities. Whether you’re looking for a casual snack or a full meal, our café offers a comfortable and welcoming atmosphere.
  • Refreshment Stands: Unwind at our refreshment stands, where you can enjoy a variety of drinks, including coffee, tea, and soft drinks. Many stands offer outdoor seating with pleasant views of the club’s activities, making it a great spot to take a break and enjoy the surroundings.

Gift Shop

  • Merchandise: Take home a piece of Fraser Trak Pony Club with our exclusive merchandise. Browse our selection of branded apparel, including shirts, hats, and jackets, as well as unique equestrian memorabilia. Our shop has something for everyone, from collectors to casual fans.
  • Gifts: Find the perfect keepsake or gift in our shop. We offer a range of items, from charming trinkets to practical souvenirs, so you can remember your visit to the pony club long after the events are over.

Pony Club Experiences

  • Stable Tours: Get an insider’s view with our exclusive stable tours. See the ponies up close, meet the trainers, and learn about the care and preparation involved. This unique experience offers a behind-the-scenes look at the world of pony riding.
  • Viewing Areas: Discover our designated viewing areas for the best perspectives of our riding arenas. Whether you prefer elevated platforms or shaded spots, our viewing areas are designed to ensure you don’t miss any of the action.

Wi-Fi and Charging Stations

  • Free Wi-Fi: Stay connected during your visit with our complimentary Wi-Fi service. Share your experiences, check event updates, or stay in touch with friends and family—all while enjoying the club’s activities.
  • Charging Stations: Keep your devices powered up with our conveniently located charging stations. Whether you need to recharge your phone or tablet, our stations ensure you stay connected and ready to capture every moment.

Event Spaces

  • Private Suites: Experience the club like never before with our private suites. These exclusive areas offer premium views, luxurious amenities, and personalized service. Perfect for special celebrations, corporate events, or an unforgettable day with friends and family.
  • Event Hosting: Make your event memorable with our versatile event spaces. From corporate gatherings to private parties, our facilities provide the ideal setting for a range of functions. Our team is dedicated to ensuring your event is a success, with tailored services and support to meet your needs.

On-Site Services

  • Information Desk: Our information desk is available to assist with any queries you may have. Whether you need directions, event details, or general assistance, our friendly staff is here to help.
  • Lost and Found: If you lose an item during your visit, report it at the information desk or contact us afterward. We will make every effort to locate and return lost items to their owners.

FAQ’S

What time should I arrive for race day?
We recommend arriving at least 30–45 minutes before the first race to find parking, explore the venue, and enjoy the pre-race atmosphere. Check the event schedule for specific race start times.
Are there any age restrictions?
All ages are welcome at Fraser Trak! However, children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult. For specific race day activities, age restrictions may apply.
Do you offer parking?
Yes, we have ample parking on-site. General parking is free, and we offer premium parking options closer to the track for those who prefer more convenience.
Is Fraser Trak family-friendly?
Absolutely! We provide family-friendly areas, activities for children, and a relaxed atmosphere where everyone can enjoy their time at the races.
Can I bring my own food and drinks?
Outside food and beverages are not allowed. However, our venue offers a variety of dining options, from quick bites to full meals, catering to all preferences.
What should I wear?
There’s no strict dress code, but many guests enjoy dressing up, especially in premium seating areas. Comfortable footwear is recommended for moving around the venue.
Is the venue accessible for individuals with disabilities?
Yes, Fraser Trak is fully accessible, with designated seating, parking, and restrooms for guests with disabilities. Please contact us in advance for any specific accommodations.
