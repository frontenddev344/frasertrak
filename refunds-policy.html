Refunds Policy

At Fraser Trak Pony Club, we strive to provide a positive experience for all our members and participants. This policy outlines our approach to refunds for memberships and event fees

Membership Fees

  • Non-Refundable: Membership fees are non-refundable once payment has been processed. This policy is in place to ensure we can maintain our facilities and programs for all members.
  • Cancellation: If you choose to cancel your membership, you will retain access to club facilities until the end of your current membership term. No refunds will be issued for any unused portion of your membership.

Event Fees

  • Refund Policy: Refund Policy: Fees for events, including riding lessons and competitions, are generally non-refundable. However, if you need to withdraw from an event due to unforeseen circumstances, please notify us at least [insert timeframe, e.g., 48 hours] before the event. Refunds may be issued at our discretion based on the circumstances
  • Event Cancellations: If an event is canceled by Fraser Trak Pony Club, all fees will be fully refunded to participants.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund for an event fee, please contact us at info@frasertrak.com with the following information:

  • Name
  • Membership or event registration number
  • Reason for refund request

Feedback and Support

We value your feedback and are here to assist you. If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Refunds Policy, please reach out to us. Your satisfaction is important to us, and we aim to provide the best possible experience at Fraser Trak Pony Club.
