Events Calendar

Find out what is coming up at Fraser Trak Pony Club, the place where exciting horseback riding takes place. Our calendar is jam-packed with activities for fans of all ages, from the always anticipated Pony Parade to thrilling trials like the Junior Jumper Show. Every event promises to be unforgettable, showcasing the special talents of young riders and their ponies. Our schedule has events for everyone, whether they are interested in show jumping or the grace of dressage. Keep track of the year's major events, memorable performances, and noteworthy occasions.

Upcoming Events

Ponies Extravaganza

The Spring Ponies Extravaganza is a vibrant showcase of young talent. Featuring various disciplines such as dressage, show jumping, and cross-country, this event highlights the skills and potential of emerging equestrians. Riders and their ponies will compete in a series of events designed to demonstrate their abilities and gain valuable experience.

Annual Pony Gala

The Annual Pony Gala is a prestigious event celebrating the achievements of riders and their ponies over the past year. The evening will include awards ceremonies, live performances, and a chance to mingle with key figures in the equestrian community. It’s an opportunity to reflect on the successes and milestones of the year in a formal and festive setting.

Youth Equestrian Development Program Launch

Join us for the launch of the Youth Equestrian Development Program, aimed at nurturing young riders. This program offers comprehensive training, mentorship, and competitive opportunities. The launch event will include demonstrations, introductory sessions, and information on how young riders can benefit from this new initiative.

Equestrian Wellness Fair

The Equestrian Wellness Fair focuses on the health and well-being of ponies. With presentations from veterinary experts, nutritionists, and farriers, attendees will gain valuable insights into maintaining optimal pony health. The fair will feature interactive sessions and exhibits on the latest in equine care and wellness.

Equestrian Fashion Show

The Equestrian Fashion Show will highlight the latest trends in riding attire and pony accessories. Designers and brands will present their newest collections, offering a glimpse into current styles and innovations. This event is perfect for those interested in combining function with fashion in the equestrian world.

Pony Art and Craft Fair

The Pony Art and Craft Fair invites participants to engage in creative activities centered around ponies. From painting and jewelry making to crafting, this event celebrates the artistic side of the equestrian community. It’s a fun and engaging way for riders and enthusiasts to showcase their creativity.

Charity Auction for Equine Welfare

Attend the Charity Auction at Fraser Trak, where a range of items and experiences will be auctioned to support equine welfare organizations. The event will feature contributions from the equestrian community, with all proceeds going to initiatives aimed at improving the lives of horses and ponies.

Historical Pony Parade

The Historical Pony Parade celebrates the rich traditions of equestrian sports. Riders and ponies will dress in historical attire, offering a visual journey through the evolution of equestrian fashion and practices. This event is a tribute to the heritage and history of pony sports.

Twilight Trail Ride

Experience the beauty of Fraser Trak’s trails during the Twilight Trail Ride. This guided event takes place during the serene hours of dusk, providing a peaceful and scenic riding experience. It’s a perfect opportunity to enjoy nature and equestrian activity in a relaxed setting.

Pony Club Family Picnic

The Pony Club Family Picnic is a relaxed and enjoyable event for members and their families. With picnic food, games, and social activities, it’s a great chance to connect with fellow members and enjoy a day of fun outside the formal competition setting.

Pony Adventure Course

The Pony Adventure Course features a series of engaging obstacles for riders and their ponies to navigate. This event emphasizes agility, precision, and teamwork, offering a playful and skill-building experience for participants of all ages.

Equine Knowledge Seminar

The Equine Knowledge Seminar offers expert insights into various equine topics, including training techniques, pony behavior, and competitive strategies. This educational event is designed for riders and enthusiasts who wish to deepen their understanding of equine sports.

Speed Surge Sprint

The Speed Surge Sprint is a high-energy, short-distance race focusing on speed and agility. This fast-paced event showcases the explosive power of ponies as they race from start to finish, providing an exhilarating experience for spectators.

Emerald Trail Challenge

The Emerald Trail Challenge features a rugged course with various obstacles, offering a dynamic and exciting test of agility and skill. This event is popular among fans of obstacle racing and provides continuous action throughout the competition.

Classic Heritage Handicap

The Classic Heritage Handicap combines tradition with competitive racing, where weights are assigned to level the playing field. This event highlights the skill of riders and ponies as they compete in a race that celebrates both history and sportsmanship.

FAQ’S

What time should I arrive for club events?
We recommend arriving at least 30–45 minutes before the start of the first event. This allows you time to find parking, explore the venue, and soak in the pre-event atmosphere. Check the club’s schedule for specific start times.
Are there any age restrictions?
All ages are welcome at our equine club! However, children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult. Certain activities may have specific age requirements.
Do you offer parking?
Yes, we provide ample on-site parking. General parking is free, with premium options available closer to the venue for added convenience.
Is the equine club family-friendly?
Absolutely! We offer family-friendly areas and activities for children, ensuring a welcoming environment where everyone can enjoy their time at the club.
Can I bring my own food and drinks?
Outside food and beverages are not permitted. However, our venue features a range of dining options, from snacks to full meals, to cater to all tastes.
What should I wear?
There is no strict dress code, though many guests choose to dress up, particularly in premium areas. We recommend wearing comfortable footwear for moving around the venue.
Is the venue accessible for individuals with disabilities?
Yes, our club is fully accessible, with designated seating, parking, and restrooms for guests with disabilities. Please reach out to us in advance if you need any specific accommodations.
